# 🌸 Genki Dojo（元気道場）

A calm, modern Japanese study app inspired by **Genki I**.

Genki Dojo brings together lessons, kana, vocabulary, grammar, exercises, speaking practice, and survival Japanese in one lightweight study space.

**Live app:** https://genki-dojo.netlify.app/

---

## Features

### Structured lesson study
- 12 Genki I lesson sections
- Grammar notes and examples
- Vocabulary review by lesson
- Practice exercises inside each lesson
- Speaking practice phrases for each lesson

### Kana practice
- Full hiragana chart
- Full katakana chart
- Tap-to-mark learned characters
- Flashcard-style kana review
- Streak tracking during practice

### Vocabulary study
- Lesson-based vocabulary flashcards
- Full lesson vocabulary lists
- Additional study vocabulary for early lessons
- Tap-to-reveal review flow

### Grammar-linked exercises
- Multiple choice
- Sentence writing
- Sentence completion
- Clickable grammar cards that open practice for a specific grammar point

### Speaking practice
- Browser-based Japanese speech recognition
- Lesson phrase practice
- Survival phrase practice
- Instant spoken comparison feedback

> Best speech-recognition support is currently in **Google Chrome**.

### Survival Japanese
- Travel-friendly phrase categories
- Convenience store, restaurant, train, shopping, hotel, emergency, and temple phrases
- Designed for practical Japan travel use

### Installable PWA
- Install to your phone or desktop like an app
- Works offline after caching
- Lightweight and fast
